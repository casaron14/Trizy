Meet Trizy Magno

Antony Polycarp Mlelwa, widely known as Trizy Magno, is an accomplished educator and blockchain enthusiast with over years of experience in the blockchain and digital asset industry. He has a proven track record in simplifying complex crypto concepts, making them accessible to a wide audience, from curious beginners to aspiring professionals.

Antony Polycarp Mlelwa has taken his mission to the global stage, appearing at major international events to champion responsible crypto adoption. He has been a featured speaker and educator at events such as Crypto Expo 2025 and other leading blockchain conferences, where he engages diverse audiences on topics ranging from digital asset safety to the transformative role of blockchain in emerging economies.
